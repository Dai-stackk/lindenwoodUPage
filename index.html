Life Starts Here!!

Student Life at Lindenwood University At Lindenwood University, student life is vibrant, diverse, and filled with opportunities for growth and connection. Our campus is a thriving hub where students can engage in a variety of activities that enrich their academic experience and foster lifelong friendships. With over 100 student-led clubs and organizations, there’s something for everyone! Whether you’re interested in academic societies, cultural groups, or hobby clubs, you’ll find a community that shares your passions. Get involved, develop your leadership skills, and make lasting memories! Lindenwood University hosts a wide array of events throughout the year, from exciting cultural festivals to engaging guest lectures. Our popular “Lynx Connect” series brings together students, faculty, and industry leaders for networking and professional development. Don’t miss out on the annual Spring Fling, a celebration of student creativity and talent! Our comfortable and modern residence halls offer a welcoming home away from home. With various living options and vibrant common spaces, you’ll quickly find your community. Enjoy a diverse dining experience with meals that cater to all tastes and dietary preferences at our dining facilities, including our popular Campus Cafe! Stay active with our NCAA Division III sports teams or join one of our many intramural leagues. Whether you’re a seasoned athlete or just looking to have fun, our state-of-the-art fitness center and recreational facilities provide endless opportunities for exercise and team spirit. Lindenwood University prioritizes your well-being. Our dedicated counseling services, academic advising, and wellness programs ensure you have the support you need to thrive both academically and personally. Join us at Lindenwood University, where student life is more than just classes—it’s about creating unforgettable experiences, building connections, and discovering your path!
